bdo : how to start

Balenos Boxes : The best way to get billions

BDO is a game that allows you to get different type of work called "life skill". When you reach a certain level of skill, you will be able to craft and sell your dishes.

1. Introduction

If you have as a goal to craft balenos boxes and sell them every day, you need to know a lot of different things. Balenos Boxes are made of 5x Balenos Meals. To craft meals you must be at least Master 1 in cooking and to put them into the boxes, you need to be at least Guru 1 . Let's now see what you will need to do to get there.

2. What skill you should up

Here is a look of which skill you should up to get the balenos boxes :

Gather : You will need to gather a big amount of meat. The higher rank you get, the biggest amourt of meat you'll make. Master 1 is a good start.

Cooking : As we say, you we'll need to get to Guru 1.

Processing : This skill will up on it's own.

Farming : This skill will up on it's own. Artisan 1 is required to get the best seeds.

3. Fast exp your skills

First of all, I recommend you to do your like skill on a Shaï. This character as a passive buff for gathering and gain some weight every single level. A Shaï level 60 will be enough.

3.1 Gathering Exp

The fastest way to level up your gathering level is to go to BEHR. At the south of the node, on the main road, you will find a big amount of weeds to gather that will increase your skill really fast. In 3 to 4 hours, you will go from beginner 1 to Artisan 6/7 with life's buff.

3.2 Cooking Exp

To get your Balenos Meal, you will need to cook a lot of different dishes. Therefore, your cooking experience will increase by itself. However, if you don't want to start as beginner 1, we do advise to craft around 100 000 beer. This will take you to Artisan 1 at least.

4. Skill Gear

There is two gear that you can have. One that is "low cost" and easy to craft and to enhance and one that is expensive and hard to get. Here is a look of both.

Loggia Gear

Loggia is a stuff that you can buy from a NPC at loggia farm next to velia. Therefore, you can enhance it easily.

Manos gear

Manos gear is really hard to enhance and you'll need memory fragment to repair it.
